About Us

Introducing the Deakin Detonator Toolkit (DDT) – the cutting-edge arsenal for modern penetration testers. Born from the innovative minds at PT-GUI, DDT is more than just a toolkit; it's a revolution in cybersecurity practice. Harnessing the robust Tauri framework alongside React and Mantine, DDT offers an intuitive interface that simplifies complex pen-testing workflows. With Python at its core, DDT automates and executes scripts for vulnerability exploitation with precision, freeing you from the intricacies of command-line tools. By the end of T2 2023, DDT boasts an impressive repertoire of 44 pen-testing tools, 5 targeted attack vectors, 12 walkthroughs of HackTheBox challenges, and a comprehensive reference for all dependencies, making it an indispensable tool for professionals and enthusiasts alike.
