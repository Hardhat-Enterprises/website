About Deakin Detonator Toolkit

The Deakin Detonator Toolkit (DDT) by PT-GUI is a cutting-edge penetration testing GUI for modern cybersecurity professionals. Built with the Tauri framework, React, and Mantine, DDT offers an intuitive interface that simplifies complex pen-testing workflows. Powered by Python, it automates vulnerability exploitation with precision, featuring 44 ethical hacking tools, 5 attack vectors, 12 HackTheBox challenge walkthroughs, and comprehensive dependency references as of T2 2023—perfect for beginners and experts alike.
